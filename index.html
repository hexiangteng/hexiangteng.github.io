About me

I'm pursuing my PhD degree in Stanford University as a member of the Stanford Vision and Learning Lab. I'm co-advised by Dr. Juan Carlos Niebles and Prof. Silvio Savarese.

My research interests include human activity understanding, video analysis, and broader computer vision and machine learning.
