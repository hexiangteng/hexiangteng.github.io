About Me

I am a 5th year CS Ph.D. student at Peking University, advised by Yuxin Peng. I do work in computer vision, with a focus on fine-grained recognition, and further interests in deep learning. I has published 9 regular papers in top-level IEEE Trans. and CCF rank-A papers, including IJCV, IEEE TIP, IEEE TCSVT, CVPR, ACM MM, IJCAI and AAAI. I was awarded Baidu Scholarship and National Scholarship in 2018.
